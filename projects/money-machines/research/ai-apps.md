## Revenue Models That Work

### 1. Freemium
- Free tier (limited usage)
- Premium tier ($20-99/mo)
- **Conversion:** 2-5% of free users upgrade

### 2. Pure Subscription
- Start at $9-29/mo
- Pro tier $49-99/mo
- Enterprise custom pricing

### 3. Credits/Usage-Based
- Pay per AI generation
- **Example:** $10 = 100 credits
- Good for infrequent users

### 4. Lifetime Deals (Launch Only)
- One-time payment ($99-299)
- Great for initial cash flow
- AppSumo, ProductHunt launches

## Solo Dev Success Formula

**From Reddit $20K MRR founder:**
1. Pick a **tiny niche** (don't compete with giants)
2. **Ship fast** (MVP in weeks, not months)
3. **Zero ads** (SEO, content, community instead)
4. **Talk to users** (build what they actually want)
5. **Double down** on what works (kill what doesn't)

**From AI SaaS playbook:**
1. Use AI to build AI tools (meta!)
2. Niche down until you're the ONLY solution
3. Charge more than you think (premium positioning)
4. Focus on MRR, not one-time sales
5. Automate everything except customer support
