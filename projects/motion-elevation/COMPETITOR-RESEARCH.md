## Market Context

The DACH automation agency market is dominated by:
1. **Enterprise-focused agencies** (€5k–€50k+ project costs — way out of reach for solopreneurs)
2. **Freelance Zapier/Make consultants** (one-off builds, no recurring support, no delivery promise)
3. **SaaS tools** (Zapier, Make, n8n — powerful but DIY, requires time and technical knowledge)

**The gap Motion Elevation fills:** No one is doing done-for-you, hosted, recurring automation for under €200/month with a 48h delivery guarantee targeting solopreneurs and small businesses in German-speaking markets.

## Competitor 3: Zapier / Make.com (the DIY Tools)

**Website:** zapier.com, make.com  
**Country:** USA (but widely used in DACH)  
**Positioning:** "Automate your work" / "Work less, achieve more"

**What They Do:**
- No-code/low-code automation platforms
- Users build their own workflows ("Zaps" / "Scenarios")
- 5,000+ app integrations
- Monthly subscription (Zapier Free → $299/month; Make Free → €29/month for 10k operations)

**Pricing:**
- Zapier: Free (5 Zaps, 100 tasks/month) → $19.99/month → $299/month
- Make: Free (1,000 ops/month) → €9/month → €29/month+

**Strengths:**
- Massive brand awareness in Germany
- Extensive integrations
- Relatively affordable entry point
- Self-service — no human needed

**Weaknesses:**
- **DIY = TIME.** Someone has to build, test, debug, maintain every Zap
- When something breaks, you fix it (or pay someone to)
- No German-language support focus
- No "done for you" — the entire value proposition requires the user to be technical
- Zapier pricing jumps steeply once you need anything real
- No one holds your hand — for a busy freelancer, this is the blocker

**Motion Elevation's Advantage vs. this competitor:**
→ We build it. They don't.
→ We maintain it. They don't.
→ When something breaks, we fix it. For them, that's your problem.
→ The hidden cost of Zapier: 10h of your time to set it up = €200+ at €20/h. Our service costs €100.

**Type:** Solo consultants, often positioning around "digitalization consulting"  
**Country:** Germany/Austria  
**Positioning:** "We help SMEs go digital"

**What They Do:**
- Strategy consulting for digital transformation
- Sometimes implementation, often just advice
- Microsoft 365 migrations, ERP implementations, "process optimization"
- Funded through BAFA consulting grants (German government subsidies)

**Pricing:**
- €150–€300/hour consulting
- Projects: €5,000–€30,000

**Strengths:**

- German-speaking, GDPR-aware

**Weaknesses:**
- Consultants advise. They don't build.
- Way too expensive for solopreneurs and small freelancers
- Bureaucratic — projects take months
- Often tech-agnostic (don't specialize in n8n/automation)
- No standardized delivery or product

**Motion Elevation's Advantage vs. this competitor:**
→ We build fast (48h vs. months)
→ We're affordable (€100/month vs. €5,000 projects)
→ We have a product, not just advice
→ We're not dependent on government subsidies or bureaucracy
