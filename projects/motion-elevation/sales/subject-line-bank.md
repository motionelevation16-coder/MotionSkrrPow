## SUBJECT LINE PRINCIPLES

| Principle | Example |
|-----------|---------|
| **Curiosity gap** | "48 Stunden bis zum ersten funktionierenden Workflow" |
| **Specific numbers** | "3 Automatisierungen, die sofort Zeit sparen" |
| **Direct question** | "Wie viele Stunden verlierst du diese Woche?" |
| **Social proof hint** | "Deine Konkurrenz automatisiert bereits" |
| **Personal/conversational** | "Kurze Frage zu {{company_name}}" |
| **Outcome-focused** | "5 hours/week back — interested?" |

> **Rule of thumb:** Short wins. Under 50 characters = better mobile open rates. Test, iterate, drop what doesn't work.
