# Motion Elevation — Funnel Blueprint
_Built for: Lyubo | Date: 2026-03-05_

---

## The Core Insight (Read This First)

Most automation agencies say "we save you time." Boring. Everyone says that.

Motion Elevation's funnel does something different: **it proves the product before they pay.**

The quiz funnel IS itself an automation. It takes their input, processes it, and spits out a personalized report — instantly. Before they've paid a cent, they've already experienced exactly what Motion Elevation does. The product sells itself by existing.

---

## Stage 1 — TOP OF FUNNEL: The Hook

**Entry points:**
- Instagram Reels / TikTok: "How many hours does your business waste on tasks a robot could do?" (no CTA, just the question — curiosity does the work)
- Reddit replies in business/entrepreneur subs: same question, link to quiz
- Affiliate posts: partners post their own takes, all link to the same quiz

**The genius:** You never sell in the content. You just make people feel the pain. The quiz is the bridge.

---

## Stage 2 — THE QUIZ: "The Automation Audit"

**URL:** motionelevation.com/audit (or similar)

**Headline:** *"Find out how much money you're leaving on the table every month"*

**Sub:** Takes 90 seconds. Get your free Automation Score.

---

### The 6 Questions:

**Q1:** What type of business do you run?
- Freelancer / Solo
- Small team (2-10)
- Agency
- Local business (café, clinic, salon, etc.)
- E-commerce

**Q2:** How many hours per week does your team spend on repetitive tasks? (emails, reports, data entry, scheduling)
- Less than 5h
- 5–15h
- 15–30h
- More than 30h

**Q3:** Which of these is your biggest time drain?
- Customer support & emails
- Invoicing / admin / reporting
- Social media & content scheduling
- Lead follow-up & sales
- Internal team coordination

**Q4:** Do you currently use any automation tools?
- No, everything is manual
- A little (basic stuff like Zapier)
- Yes, but they're clunky or unreliable
- Yes, I'm already well-automated

**Q5:** What's your monthly revenue range?
- Under €2k
- €2k–€10k
- €10k–€50k
- €50k+

**Q6:** What's your #1 goal right now?
- Save time
- Scale without hiring
- Look more professional to clients
- Just curious

---

### What Happens After They Submit:

They instantly get their **Automation Score (0–100)** + a personalized breakdown:

**Score 0–30 → "Manual Mode"**
> "You're running your business like it's 2010. You're losing an estimated X hours/month to tasks that take a bot 3 seconds. Here's your priority automation plan..."

**Score 31–60 → "Half-Automated"**
> "You've started, but you're leaving the heavy stuff on the table. The tasks killing your growth are [specific answer from Q3]. Here's what to fix first..."

**Score 61–85 → "Almost There"**
> "You're doing well — but your automations aren't talking to each other. You're still losing time in the gaps. Here's the missing layer..."

**Score 86–100 → "Automation Pro"**
> "Impressive. But even pros have blind spots. Here's where your setup probably leaks money..."

**Each result includes:**
- Their top 3 automations to implement (based on Q3 + Q1)
- Estimated hours saved per month
- Estimated money saved (hours × their hourly rate, calculated from revenue ÷ 160h)
- A direct CTA: "Want these built for you in 48h?"

## Stage 3 — EMAIL SEQUENCE: The 5-Day Close

They enter their email to get the full report. Now the sequence runs automatically.

**Day 0 (instant):** Full personalized report + "Your top 3 automations" PDF
> Subject: *Your Automation Score: [X]/100*

**Day 1:** Case study — a business exactly like theirs, before/after
> Subject: *How a [their business type] owner got 12 hours back every week*

**Day 2:** The "what's actually inside" email — show the n8n workflows, the integrations, the real stuff
> Subject: *What you actually get (no fluff)*
> This is where you SHOW the product. Screenshots of workflows. Real automations. Proof it works.

**Day 3:** The math email — ROI calculator
> Subject: *Is €100/month worth it? Let's do the math.*
> If they save 10h/month and their time is worth €30/h = €300 saved. They pay €100. Net gain: €200/month.

**Day 4:** Objection killer
> Subject: *"But I'm not technical"*
> Address the top 3 objections: not technical enough, already tried Zapier, too busy to set it up.
> Answer: you don't set it up. We do. In 48h.

**Day 5:** Soft deadline + affiliate mention
> Subject: *Last thing (+ a way to get this for free)*
> CTA to subscribe + introduce the affiliate option: "Refer 1 client → get a month free. Refer 5 → covered forever."

## Stage 4 — SALES PAGE: The One-Pager

**Structure (scroll order):**

1. **Hero:** "Your business runs on autopilot. Or it doesn't." — CTA: Take the Audit
2. **Pain:** The hidden cost of manual work (the math, made personal with their quiz score if they came from the quiz)
3. **Solution:** What Motion Elevation is (3 sentences, no jargon)
4. **What's included:** The automations library — customer support, invoicing, lead follow-up, etc.
5. **How it works:** 3 steps — Take the audit → We build your stack → You save time
6. **Proof:** Case studies / testimonials (build these with the first 3 clients, even if free/discounted)
7. **Pricing:** €100/month OR €500 lifetime (framed as: "skip 5 months, own it forever")
8. **The Affiliate Hook:** "Turn your subscription into income" — 15% per referral, uncapped
9. **FAQ:** Addresses the 5 most common objections
10. **Final CTA:** "Start your automation audit" (loops back to quiz)

**The genius of the loop:** Every exit point on the sales page sends them back to the quiz if they're not ready. The quiz re-segments them. The sequence re-runs. No lead dies.

## Stage 5 — THE AFFILIATE FLYWHEEL

This is the part that makes it compound.

**How it works:**
- Any subscriber can become an affiliate
- They get a unique link that tracks their referrals
- 15% recurring commission per referral (so €15/month per client they bring in)
- Refer 7 clients → their own subscription is free + they're making money

**Why it's not MLM:**
- Commission only on direct sales — no downline, no recruitment bonus
- The product has to actually work or affiliates stop selling
- No mandatory buy-in to participate
- Affiliates earn from real business value delivered, not from recruiting

**The "skin in the game" effect:**
When someone pays €500 lifetime, they're now motivated to refer people — because they want to feel like they got a deal AND they can earn back that €500 through referrals. 3–4 referrals and they're in profit. They become your best salespeople without you paying a salary.

**Affiliate onboarding (automated, of course):**
1. They subscribe → immediately get affiliate link + promo kit (email templates, caption ideas, story templates)
2. n8n tracks referral → Telegram notification to Lyubo → auto-welcome email to new client
3. Monthly: affiliate gets a performance report (how many clicks, conversions, earnings)
4. Milestone emails: "You've earned €50 this month 🎉 — here's your next tier"

## Stage 6 — ONBOARDING (The 48h Promise)

Once they pay, the clock starts. This is where you deliver and build trust.

**Hour 0:** Automated welcome email + onboarding form (what tools do you use? what's your first automation priority?)
**Hour 2:** Lyubo reviews form (or Motion reviews it eventually) — picks top automation to build first
**Hour 24:** First automation live + Loom walkthrough sent
**Hour 48:** Full stack delivered + "here's your control panel" (their webhook URLs, how to trigger things)

The 48h delivery promise is the hook that makes people talk about you.
