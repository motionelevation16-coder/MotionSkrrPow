## The 5 Angles

### 1. Niche API Products *(You Already Started — Wrong Execution)*
- HookScore + PredMarket = right instinct, wrong distribution (RapidAPI is a graveyard)
- The play: find a painful data gap in a specific industry, build a clean API, distribute through communities where those people already hang out
- **German edge:** You speak German → can scrape/aggregate German-language sources English devs can't touch. German business data is fragmented and poorly digitized = opportunity.
- Ask xHeal senior devs for architecture advice — you're already in the room with the right people
- Price: €29-99/month

### 2. Micro-SaaS for Specific Workflows
- NOT a full SaaS company. ONE feature, ONE workflow, ONE defined group
- Example: WhatsApp auto-responder for German Handwerker (productized version of what ME delivers manually)
- **The flywheel:** ME clients = beta testers → validate what works → package most popular as self-serve tool → €19-49/month
- Service revenue → product revenue. Scales without your time.
- You'll KNOW what people need because you've already built it for them

### 3. Lead Intelligence for Underserved DACH Market
- US has Apollo, ZoomInfo, Clearbit. German SMBs have almost nothing comparable.
- Build scraper: Google Maps + MyHammer + industry directories → enrich with email/phone/website/rating → weekly lists to subscribers
- Sell to: marketing agencies, B2B sales teams, insurance brokers targeting tradespeople
- `lead_finder.py` is already the seed of this
- Price: €49-149/month per city/niche combination
- GDPR-compliant packaging is the differentiator

### 4. Automation Templates Marketplace
- 19 polished n8n workflow JSONs already exist
- n8n users are technical but lazy — they'll pay €29-79 per workflow to skip the build
- The documentation (README + setup guides + credential instructions + Sheet schemas) IS the product
- Platform: Gumroad or Lemonsqueezy
- Start with Handwerk bundle, expand to other niches
- Build once, sell forever

### 5. The "AI Intern" Play — Compound Knowledge
- Not freelancing. Positioning.
- Skill stack building: technical automation + sales/marketing instinct + German market access + AI-native design
- In 6 months → fractional automation/growth person for 2-3 small companies
- €500-1,500/month per company for 5-10 hours
- xHeal senior devs = technical knowledge source
- Miami sales guy = learn how to position and sell this
