## The Problem with Variable Pricing

Lyubo's concern is valid: selling the same product at different prices looks unserious.

### Options:

**Option A: Single Global Price**
- Pick one number: **€297/month** or **€349/month**
- Simple, clean, professional
- Disadvantage: Too expensive for Spain/Portugal, too cheap for Germany/Netherlands

**Option B: Tiered by Region**
- Western Europe + US/UK/AU: €397/month
- Southern/Eastern Europe: €247/month
- Clear reasoning: cost of living adjustment
- Disadvantage: Still variable

**Option C: Value-Based Tiers (Recommended)**
- **Starter:** €197/month — 1 automation, basic support
- **Growth:** €397/month — 3 automations, priority support
- **Scale:** €797/month — Unlimited automations, dedicated slack, 24h delivery

This way price varies by VALUE, not geography. Everyone sees the same menu.

**Option D: Per-Automation Pricing**
- €97-197 per automation (one-time)
- Plus €47/month maintenance
- More transactional, less recurring

---

## Recommendation

**Go with Option C (Value-Based Tiers)** with these numbers:

| Tier | Price | What's Included |
|------|-------|-----------------|
| Starter | €197/mo | 1 automation, email support, 48h delivery |
| Growth | €397/mo | 3 automations, priority support, 24h delivery |
| Scale | €797/mo | Unlimited, dedicated channel, same-day delivery |

**Why this works:**
1. Same pricing globally — no awkward regional adjustments
2. Clear value ladder — clients self-select
3. €197 starter is close to 1/5 minimum wage for most of Europe
4. Recurring revenue model
5. Room to upsell

## Alternative Strategies

### 1. Anchor High, Discount
- List price €597/month
- "Launch price" €297/month
- Creates urgency + perceived value

### 2. Pay-Per-Result
- Only pay when automation saves you X hours
- Risky but differentiating

### 3. One-Time + Retainer
- €497 setup + €97/month maintenance
- Lower monthly commitment, higher upfront

### 4. Freemium
- 1 free automation forever
- Paid for additional
- Good for lead gen, harder to monetize

---

## Final Recommendation

**Start with Option C (Value Tiers) at €197/€397/€797**

Test for 30 days. If conversion is low in Spain/Portugal, consider regional discounts later. But start clean and simple.
