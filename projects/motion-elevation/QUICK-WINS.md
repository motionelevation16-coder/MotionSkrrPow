## TODAY (Next 4 Hours)

### 1. DM 5 people in your existing network RIGHT NOW
**Time:** 30 minutes  
**Revenue potential:** €100–500 (one client)

You know people. Not hundreds — just a few. Someone who runs a small business, freelances, manages a local shop, has a side hustle.

Send THIS message (WhatsApp, Instagram, whatever):

> Hey [Name]! Ich starte gerade einen Service wo ich für Selbstständige Automationen aufsetze — also Dinge wie Rechnungserinnerungen, Lead-Follow-ups, E-Mail-Sortierung. Komplett fertig aufgesetzt in 48h.
>
> Suche gerade meine ersten 3 Kunden die das testen wollen, gerne zu einem Freundschaftspreis. Hast du sowas in deinem Business wo du Zeit verlierst?

Do not overthink the wording. Send it messy. The goal is a reply, not a perfect pitch.

## THIS WEEK (Zero Budget, High Impact)

### 6. Build One "Proof of Concept" Automation for Yourself
**Time:** 2–3 hours  
**Revenue potential:** Massive (it becomes your demo)

Build one automation in n8n that you actually use. Example:
- Auto-reply to new contacts who fill your quiz with their score
- Auto-log all your daily tasks to a Google Sheet
- Auto-send yourself a digest of overnight emails

Then record a 60-second screen recording of it running. That becomes Reel #1, the demo you send in DMs, your portfolio.

A working automation beats 1,000 words of explanation.

## What NOT To Do This Week

- ❌ Don't spend time designing a logo before you have a client
- ❌ Don't build a perfect website before you have testimonials
- ❌ Don't wait until the email sequence is "perfect" before sending DMs
- ❌ Don't post 10 pieces of content before you've talked to 5 real people
- ❌ Don't build automations for clients who haven't paid yet (unless it's explicitly the free beta deal)
- ❌ Don't register a company until you have consistent revenue (Einzelunternehmer is fine for now)

Ship ugly. Talk to people. Build for paying clients only.
