## Cold Call Script

**Opening:**
"Hi, is this [Name]? This is [Your Name]. I work with dental practices on their phone systems. Do you have 30 seconds?"

**If yes:**
"Quick question — roughly how many calls would you say go to voicemail at your practice each week? During lunch, after hours, busy times?"

**Let them answer, then:**
"Got it. The reason I'm calling is we've built an AI receptionist specifically for dental offices. It answers every call, books appointments into your calendar, handles the basic questions — and costs less than a part-time hire."

"I'm not asking you to buy anything today. But I can show you exactly how it works in about 15 minutes. Would [Tuesday at 2pm] or [Wednesday at 10am] work better for a quick demo?"

**If objection - "We're fine":**
"Totally understand. Out of curiosity, what happens when all your lines are busy or someone calls at 8pm with a toothache? Just want to make sure there's nothing here that could help."

**If objection - "Send me info":**
"Happy to. What's the best email? Also — the demo only takes 15 minutes and you can actually call the AI yourself. Would it be worth a quick look before you decide? I have [time] open."

## Demo Meeting Agenda (15 minutes)

1. **Intro** (2 min)
   - Thank them for their time
   - Quick overview of what you'll show

2. **Their Situation** (3 min)
   - How many calls/day?
   - What happens after hours?
   - Biggest front desk challenge?

3. **Live Demo** (5 min)
   - Call the demo number together
   - Show booking flow
   - Show the dashboard

4. **ROI Discussion** (3 min)
   - "If this helps you book just 5 more patients/month..."
   - Show pricing vs. their cost of missed calls

5. **Next Steps** (2 min)
   - "Want to try it for 2 weeks, free?"
   - Get calendar booked for setup call
