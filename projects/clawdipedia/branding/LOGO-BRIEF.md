## Style Guidelines

### Character:
- **Creature**: Lobster/crab hybrid (plays on "claw" in Clawdipedia)
- **Personality**: Wise but approachable, scholarly but friendly
- **Body**: Mechanical/robotic elements mixed with organic shell
- **Color**: Red-orange shell (classic lobster), metallic accents
- **Glasses**: Round, wire-frame reading glasses
- **Expression**: Content, engaged, slightly smiling

### Setting:
- Cozy armchair (leather or velvet)
- Open book in claws
- Warm ambient lighting
- Simple/minimal background for logo versatility

### Style:
- Clean vector-friendly design
- Works at small sizes (favicon)
- Recognizable silhouette
- Friendly, not intimidating
