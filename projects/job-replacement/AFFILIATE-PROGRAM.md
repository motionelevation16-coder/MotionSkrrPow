# Affiliate/Reseller Program Structure 🤝

*Turn customers into salespeople. Zero ad spend. Infinite reach.*

---

## Why an Affiliate Program?

1. **Zero upfront cost** — Pay only when sales happen
2. **Scales infinitely** — More affiliates = more reach
3. **Builds community** — Affiliates become advocates
4. **Recurring revenue sharing** — Keeps affiliates motivated
5. **Social proof** — Word-of-mouth > ads

---

## Program Tiers

### Tier 1: Ambassador (Free)

**Who:** Anyone who wants to refer casually

**Commission:**
- **20% of first month** on any referred client
- **One-time payout** per referral

**Requirements:**
- Sign up with email
- Get unique referral link
- Share with network

**Payout:**
- Minimum $50 to withdraw
- Paid via PayPal/Wise monthly

**Best for:** Happy customers, social media followers, casual referrers

---

### Tier 2: Partner ($49/year or 3 referrals)

**Who:** Active promoters, small influencers, agencies

**Commission:**
- **25% recurring** for 12 months
- Earn every month client stays

**Benefits:**
- Priority support
- Early access to new features
- Co-branded marketing materials
- Monthly partner newsletter

**Requirements:**
- $49/year OR 3 successful referrals
- Basic vetting (no spam, quality leads)

**Payout:**
- No minimum
- Paid via PayPal/Wise monthly

**Best for:** Agency owners, consultants, influencers with audience

---

### Tier 3: Reseller (Application Only)

**Who:** Agencies, consultants who want to white-label

**Commission:**
- **30-40% recurring** (negotiable)
- OR wholesale pricing (you set your price)

**Benefits:**
- White-label option (your brand)
- Dedicated account manager
- Custom landing pages
- Bulk onboarding support
- Priority feature requests

**Requirements:**
- Application + call
- Minimum 5 clients/quarter expected
- Professional online presence

**Payout:**
- Net 15 (faster payouts)
- Wire, PayPal, Wise, Crypto

**Best for:** Marketing agencies, business consultants, VA services

## Program Structure

### Referral Tracking

```
Affiliate signs up
    ↓
Gets unique link: yourdomain.com/?ref=AFFILIATE123
    ↓
Link sets 90-day cookie on visitor
    ↓
Visitor signs up → attributed to affiliate
    ↓
Client pays → Commission calculated
    ↓
Payout on monthly cycle
```

### Cookie Duration
- **90 days** — Industry standard, fair to affiliates
- Last-click attribution (whoever referred last gets credit)

### Commission Calculation

**Example (Partner tier):**
```
Client signs up for $697/month Growth plan
→ Affiliate earns 25% = $174.25/month
→ For 12 months = $2,091 total potential

If client stays 6 months:
→ Affiliate earns: 6 × $174.25 = $1,045.50
```

## Terms & Conditions (Key Points)

1. **Payouts:** Monthly, net 30, minimum $50 (Ambassador) or $0 (Partner)
2. **Refunds:** If client refunds within 30 days, commission clawed back
3. **Prohibited:** Spam, misleading claims, paid ads on our brand name
4. **Termination:** Either party can end with 30 days notice
5. **Exclusivity:** None — affiliates can promote competitors
6. **Changes:** We can modify commission rates with 30 days notice

## FAQ (For Affiliate Page)

**Q: How much can I earn?**
A: Ambassador: 20% of first month. Partner: 25% recurring for 12 months. Top partners earn $1,000+ monthly.

**Q: When do I get paid?**
A: Monthly, around the 15th. Minimum $50 (Ambassador) or no minimum (Partner).

**Q: How long does the cookie last?**
A: 90 days. If someone clicks your link and signs up within 90 days, you get credit.

**Q: Can I promote on paid ads?**
A: Yes, but you cannot bid on our brand name.

**Q: What if a referral asks for a refund?**
A: If refund happens within 30 days, commission is reversed. After 30 days, you keep it.
