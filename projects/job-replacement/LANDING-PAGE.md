## Design Notes for Carrd

### Colors (Suggestion)
- **Background:** Off-white or very light gray (#FAFAFA)
- **Text:** Near-black (#1A1A1A)
- **Accent:** Electric blue (#2563EB) or bold orange (#F97316)
- **Buttons:** Solid accent color, white text

### Typography
- **Headlines:** Bold sans-serif (Inter, DM Sans, or Satoshi)
- **Body:** Regular weight, good line spacing
- **Keep it clean** — lots of white space

### Layout
- Single column, scrolling
- Each section is visually distinct
- Icons/emojis for visual breaks
- No clutter

### Images
- Optional: Abstract shapes, gradients
- Or: Real photos of tools/dashboards (later)
- Keep minimal for now
