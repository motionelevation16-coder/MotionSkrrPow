## Outreach Automation 🚀

### Cold Email System

```
Lead Source (Apollo, LinkedIn)
    ↓
Enrich (scrape website, social)
    ↓
Qualify (AI scores fit)
    ↓
Personalize (AI writes email based on research)
    ↓
Send (Instantly.ai / Smartlead)
    ↓
Track Opens/Clicks
    ↓
Follow-up Sequence (3-5 emails)
    ↓
Meeting Booked (Cal.com)
```

### Outreach Script

**Subject:** Quick question about [specific pain point]

**Body:**
```
Hey [Name],

Noticed you're posting manually to 4 platforms. That's 2+ hours/day you're not creating.

We automate the entire posting pipeline:
- AI generates posts from your long-form content
- Schedules at optimal times
- Cross-posts everywhere
- Tracks what works

One creator went from 10 hrs/week → 2 hrs/week on social.

Worth a 15-min call to see if it fits?

[Your name]

P.S. If timing's off, no worries—just curious if this is a problem you're solving.
```

### Outreach Volume

| Stage | Daily Emails | Expected Responses | Meetings/Week |
|-------|-------------|-------------------|---------------|
| Week 1-2 | 50 | 2-3 | 2-3 |
| Week 3-4 | 100 | 5-8 | 5-8 |
| Month 2+ | 200 | 10-15 | 10-15 |

**Conversion:**
- 3-5% reply rate
- 30-50% of replies book calls
- 30-40% of calls close
