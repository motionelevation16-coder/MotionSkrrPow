## High-Impact Automations

### 1. Appointment Scheduling Bot
**What it does:**
- Patients book online 24/7
- AI handles rescheduling via text/chat
- Auto-sends confirmations + reminders
- Fills cancellation gaps from waitlist
- Syncs with EHR/practice management

**Tools:** Calendly for Healthcare, Acuity, custom n8n

**Time saved:** 20-30 hrs/week

**Price to client:** $497-997/mo

---

### 2. No-Show Prevention System
**What it does:**
- Reminder sequence: 1 week, 2 days, morning of
- Confirm via text (reply "1" to confirm)
- Auto-reschedule no-confirms
- Track no-show patterns per patient
- Fill gaps from waitlist

**Tools:** Twilio, n8n, EHR API

**Time saved:** 10-15 hrs/week
**Revenue saved:** $2,000-10,000/mo (fewer no-shows)

**Price to client:** $597-997/mo

---

### 3. Patient Intake Automation
**What it does:**
- Digital forms sent before appointment
- Auto-extract data into EHR
- Verify insurance before visit
- Flag incomplete forms
- Consent management

**Tools:** JotForm/Typeform + n8n + EHR API

**Time saved:** 15-25 hrs/week

**Price to client:** $497-797/mo
