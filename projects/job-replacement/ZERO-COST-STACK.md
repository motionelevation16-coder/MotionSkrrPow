## Zero-Cost Outreach Flow

```
MANUAL LEAD FINDING (LinkedIn, Google Maps, Social)
    ↓
STORE IN GOOGLE SHEET
    ↓
FIND EMAILS (Hunter free + Snov free = 75/month)
    ↓
VERIFY (ZeroBounce 100 free)
    ↓
PERSONALIZE (Gemini free API)
    ↓
SEND VIA GMAIL/ZOHO (GMass 50/day free)
    ↓
TRACK IN HUBSPOT FREE
    ↓
BOOK VIA CALENDLY FREE
    ↓
CLOSE ON GOOGLE MEET
```

### Daily Volume (Free Tier)
- Emails found: ~3-5/day (75/month ÷ 30)
- Emails verified: ~3/day (100/month ÷ 30)
- Emails sent: 50/day (GMass limit)
- **Bottleneck:** Finding verified emails

### Solution: Manual + Warm Outreach
Instead of cold email at scale, focus on:
1. **LinkedIn DMs** — Free, unlimited, warm
2. **Twitter DMs** — Free, unlimited, warm
3. **Instagram DMs** — Free, unlimited, warm
4. **Facebook Groups** — Free, warm leads
5. **Cold email** — 50/day max (focus on highest quality)

## High-Impact Free Channels

### 1. LinkedIn Outreach (Free)
```
Daily routine (1 hour):
- Send 30 connection requests (personalized)
- Send 20 messages to existing connections
- Comment on 10 posts in target niche
- Post 1 valuable piece of content

Weekly results:
- 150 new connections
- 100 DM conversations
- 5-10 calls booked
```

### 2. Twitter/X Outreach (Free)
```
Daily routine (30 min):
- Reply to 20 tweets from target audience
- DM 10 people who engaged with your content
- Post 3-5 tweets (value + case study + CTA)

Weekly results:
- 50-100 DM conversations
- 3-5 calls booked
```

### 3. Facebook Groups (Free)
```
Daily routine (30 min):
- Join 2 new groups in target niche
- Answer 5 questions helpfully (no pitch)
- Post 1 value piece per group (weekly)
- DM people who engage

Weekly results:
- 20-30 DM conversations
- 2-3 calls booked
```

### 4. Reddit (Free)
```
Daily routine (20 min):
- Find posts asking about automation
- Give genuinely helpful answers
- DM users who seem like fits
- Post case studies in relevant subs

Weekly results:
- 5-10 quality leads
- 1-2 calls booked
```

### 5. Cold Email (50/day free)
```
Daily routine:
- Send 50 personalized emails
- Follow up on previous sends
- Handle responses

Weekly results (at 3% reply rate):
- 10 replies
- 3-4 calls booked
```

### Combined Weekly Projection
```
LinkedIn: 5-10 calls
Twitter: 3-5 calls
Facebook: 2-3 calls
Reddit: 1-2 calls
Cold Email: 3-4 calls
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Total: 14-24 calls/week
At 25% close rate: 3-6 new clients/week
At $500 avg: $1,500-3,000/week
```

## Phase Transitions

### Phase 1: $0/month (Weeks 1-4)
- Manual lead finding
- Free email tools
- Focus on warm outreach (LinkedIn, Twitter)
- Target: 5 clients, $2,500 MRR

### Phase 2: $50-100/month (Months 2-3)
**Unlock when:** $2,500+ MRR
- Instantly.ai ($37) — Scale cold email
- Better email warmup
- Target: 15 clients, $7,500 MRR

### Phase 3: $200-300/month (Months 4-6)
**Unlock when:** $7,500+ MRR
- Apollo ($99) — Lead database
- Cal.com paid — Team scheduling
- Better AI (OpenAI) — Higher quality
- Target: 30 clients, $15,000 MRR

### Phase 4: $500+/month (Month 6+)
**Unlock when:** $15,000+ MRR
- Full tool stack
- Hire VA for outreach
- Scale to 100+ clients

## The Real Unlock: Your Time

At $0 tool cost, the investment is TIME.

**Suggested daily schedule (3-4 hours):**
```
Morning (1 hour):
- LinkedIn: 30 connections + 20 DMs + 10 comments

Midday (1 hour):
- Cold email: 50 sends + response handling
- Twitter: 20 replies + 10 DMs

Evening (1 hour):
- Facebook groups: Answer questions, provide value
- Content creation: 1 post for next day
- Follow-up: Check all channels for responses
```

**At 3 hours/day, 7 days/week:**
- Week 1: Build pipeline, 2-3 calls
- Week 2: Pipeline flowing, 5-8 calls
- Week 3: Closing clients, 8-12 calls
- Week 4: First paying clients, iterate
