## First Comment / Maker Post

Hey ProductHunt 👋

I'm the solo dev behind HookScore. Here's the honest story.

I got obsessed with short-form content — specifically the hook problem. Everyone says "the first 3 seconds are everything" but there was no tooling that actually measured hook quality. You'd write something, post it, and wait 48 hours to find out if it worked.

So I built HookScore. It started as a quick weekend experiment — I just wanted to score hooks automatically before posting them. Then I added a compare endpoint so I could A/B test before publishing instead of after. Then batch scoring for when I had 10 variations. Then emoji impact analysis because I genuinely couldn't tell if emojis were helping or hurting.

Six endpoints later, I have something that I think is actually useful.

The stack is FastAPI + GPT-4o-mini + Railway. Nothing fancy. The interesting work was building the scoring rubric — figuring out what actually makes a hook land. Turns out the biggest differentiators are specificity, implied contrast, and whether the curiosity gap is earned or faked.

It's live on RapidAPI with a free tier (30 calls/month, no card). Paid plans from $3.99.

Who it's for: content creators who write hooks daily and want a second opinion, devs building content tools, agencies managing multiple accounts, and anyone wiring this into n8n or Make workflows.

Happy to answer questions. Genuinely curious what you think could be better — this is early and I'm iterating.
