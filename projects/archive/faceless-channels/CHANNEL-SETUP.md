# CROSS-CHANNEL NOTES

## Naming & Branding
- Keep character names consistent across all bios. "Rick & Morty" and "Peter & Stewie" are the personality anchors — don't dilute with generic names.
- Thumbnails/cover frames: use bold text + character silhouettes or AI-rendered stills. Clean, high-contrast. Text should be readable at 100px.

## Legal Note
These channels use copyrighted characters. Keep content clearly transformative (parody/education) and avoid any direct commercial use of official character art. Use AI-generated style-consistent artwork, not screenshots or official renders. Stay in the safe zone.

## Rendering Consistency
Reference `character-consistency.md` in this folder for AI generation prompts. Establish a locked visual style in the first 5 videos and don't change it. Viewers should recognise the channel instantly from the thumbnail alone.

## Which Channel to Prioritize First
If resources are limited: **launch Peter.Predicts first.** The audience has higher purchase intent and the monetization path is faster. API.Rick has broader top-of-funnel potential but a longer path to revenue. Run them in parallel if possible; otherwise Peter first.
