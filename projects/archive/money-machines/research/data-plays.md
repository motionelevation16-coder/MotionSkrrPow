## Business Models

### 1. **Data Products**
Sell packaged datasets to businesses:
- Market reports ($500-$5K per report)
- Trend analyses
- Targeted lead lists
- Job listing data
- Competitor intelligence

**Revenue:** One-time or subscription ($99-$999/mo per client)

### 2. **APIs (Data-as-a-Service)**
Real-time data extraction via API:
- E-commerce price monitoring
- News aggregation
- Social media sentiment
- Product availability tracking

**Revenue:** Usage-based pricing ($0.01-$0.10 per request) or tiered subscriptions ($500-$5K/mo)

### 3. **Custom Projects**
Tailored scraping solutions for specific clients:
- Niche data collection
- One-off deep dives
- Ongoing monitoring

**Revenue:** $3K-$15K per project + retainers ($1K-$5K/mo)

### 4. **Analytics SaaS**
Turn scraped data into insights dashboards:
- Price comparison tools
- Market trend trackers
- SEO monitoring platforms

**Revenue:** SaaS subscriptions ($29-$299/mo per user)

## Legal & Compliance ⚠️

### Critical Rules
1. **Respect robots.txt** — Some sites block scraping
2. **Review Terms of Service** — Many platforms prohibit scraping
3. **No personal data** — GDPR/privacy laws apply
4. **Only public data** — Don't bypass logins
5. **Transparency** — Buyers want ethical sourcing

### Compliance Framework
- Join [Ethical Web Data Collection Initiative](https://ethicalwebdata.com/) (EWDCI)
- Implement clear data sourcing policies
- KYC for buyers (know your customer)
- ISO 27001 certification (if scaling)

**Reality:** Compliance is now a **competitive advantage**, not just a checkbox.
